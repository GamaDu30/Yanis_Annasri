About Me

From the first moment I held a controller, I was entranced by the world of video games. As I traversed digital landscapes, my fascination grew into a desire to understand the magic behind the screen. This curiosity led me to coding during my teenage years. Self-taught and fueled by passion, I experimented with languages and game engines, forging a connection between my love for gaming and the art of coding. All those efforts propels me forward in my career as a video game programmer, with the ultimate goal of coding experiences that mirror the excitement and creativity of my earliest gaming moments.
